Purpose

My name is Enmanuel De los Santos, I’ve been working with computers for some time till now, and you’ll be impressed with how many problems we can solve with it. This is why I created PetZone, For those looking to buy, adopt or sell a dog, cat, horse, rabbit, fish, bird, or reptile, you are in the right place. We have done partnetship with providers with some of the best pets providers all around the world, so you can get your pets at a affordable price, or in some cases you can adopt one of the many who are looking for a home. Our goal is that you can receive what you were looking for.

Audience

As we stated before, this website is for everyone who wants to buy, adopt or sell the animals that I have mentioned before, even if you are doing this for the first time getting your first pet or you already have some time dealing with animals, this place will fit your expectations.

Home Page

What kind of audience will be on my site?

In my web site plan one of the things I stated was that my I webpage would be directed to all kind of audiences, from people who are having their first pet, or for those who have a lot of experience and just got here out of curiosity. I will make the information pretty easy-to-read in my website, thinking in those people who have not experience with their pets or are not sure whether which kind of animal they want to get.

Images for the Home page

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

Inside the website, there will be a form, where the customer will put some information about the pets(price, size, where it is located, etc.), and also, they will be able to make specific question to us, that will be answered through email.

Images for Contact Us page

Why people will visit my site?

My site will call people attention because of its simplicity. It will not have any complicated vocabulary, or some unnecessary steps that people need to go through in some other websites. Our goal is that as soon as you get in our site you can get your information in the fastest way possible, and exactly what you need. Nothing about unnecessary details, just what you need, and no more than that.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

One of our related pages will have a form, where, you will put your inquiries and get more information from us. You will receive this information via e-mail. In this form you will be asked to put your name, e-mail address and your inquiry. And we will get in touch with you as soon as possible.
