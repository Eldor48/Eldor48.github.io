Frequently asked questions


What is so special about your website?

We have a wide variety of pets, and animal's products on our website, we have a partnership with other stores and animal owners around the world so you can get that animal that you have been dreaming about.


How do I buy a pet in your store?

It is pretty simple! You just have to send us a message through our channels on social media, or if you desire, you can do contact us through email in our Contact form here inside of our webpage. We will ask you a few questions and will get you the animal that you will love and will fit your necessities.


Which payment methods are available?

We accept all cards - Maestro, Visa, American Express, Mastercard. You can either pay via our website or by phone. We also accept PayPal.


What things can I do to take care of my pet?

Feed your pet good and high-quality foods.
Take them for a walk every day for at least half an hour.
Provide them the needed vaccination on time.
Keep a clean and hygienic environment for them.
Visit the Veterinarian on a weekly/monthly basis.
Engage and do not leave them alone for a long time.
